The Quiet Morning

Published on January 2, 2026

The morning arrived without noise. No cars, no voices, just the soft light slipping through the curtains.

I sat by the window with a warm cup of tea, watching the world slowly wake up. Birds hopped from branch to branch, unbothered by time.

In that moment, everything felt simple. There was nowhere to rush, nothing to fix. Just breathing, watching, and being.
